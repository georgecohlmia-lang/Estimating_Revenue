The piece most founders skip entirely

Pipeline math tells you what's possible. Capacity tells you what's real.

Three inputs below set the cap on how many deals you can actually close in a month. We use that to adjust each scenario's revenue down to the deals you can really chase — not the ones that are theoretically open.
